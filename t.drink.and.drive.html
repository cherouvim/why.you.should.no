Why You Should Not → Drink And Drive

Don't drink and drive. It's a major cause of preventable deaths globally. Alcohol impairs judgment, coordination, and reaction time, making driving dangerous. Even a small amount increases crash risk, escalating with higher blood alcohol concentration (BAC). Besides risking a crash, you may face arrest and license loss. Always find a safe and sober way home after drinking. Use a designated driver, ride-sharing, public transport, or a taxi. Make the responsible choice—don't drink and drive—to ensure road safety for everyone.
