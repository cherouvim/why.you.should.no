Why You Should Not → Hand Guns To Children

Never give guns to children. They lack the maturity, understanding, and emotional capacity to handle weapons safely. They may accidentally harm themselves or others due to their lack of judgment. Children may act impulsively or recklessly, unable to handle the responsibility of owning a gun. They may lack the physical strength and dexterity needed to handle a firearm safely. These factors greatly increase the risk of injury or death, making it not worth the risk. Wait until they're older and properly trained in gun safety before allowing them to handle firearms.
