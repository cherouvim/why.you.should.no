Why You Should Not → Bully People

Stop bullying. It harms both the victim and the aggressor. Bullying is using power to harm others physically, verbally, or online. It's not acceptable, and steps must be taken to prevent and stop it. Bullying causes emotional distress, leading to depression, anxiety, and low self-esteem. It can also cause physical injury. Research shows bullies face future problems, struggling with relationships and the law. Treat others with kindness and respect. Stand up against bullying always.
