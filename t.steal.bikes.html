Why You Should Not → Steal Bikes

Stealing bikes, or any other property, is wrong and is against the law in most places. In addition to being illegal, stealing can also have serious consequences for the perpetrator, including fines, imprisonment, and a criminal record. A criminal record can make it difficult to find employment, housing, and other opportunities, and can have long-term negative impacts on a person's life. Stealing also causes harm to the victim, who may lose property that has sentimental or practical value, and may suffer financial or other consequences as a result of the theft. Instead of stealing, it is important to respect the property of others and to find legal and ethical ways to obtain the things you need or want.
