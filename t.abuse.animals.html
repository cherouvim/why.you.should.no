Why You Should Not → Abuse Animals

Don't abuse animals. Treat them with kindness and respect. Animal abuse is cruel, illegal, and a red flag for deeper issues like violence or lack of empathy. It's vital to address and rectify these problems for the benefit of animals and humans alike. Create a better world by being compassionate to all beings.
